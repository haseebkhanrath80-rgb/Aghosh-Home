Alkhidmat Aghosh Homes

Alkhidmat Foundation's Aghosh Homes project is the best orphanage program in Pakistan, providing unmatched boarding and lodging facilities on the pattern of a Cadet College. The project offers a safe and nurturing environment for orphaned and destitute children to thrive and achieve their full potential.

At Aghosh Homes, children live in a family-like setting with trained caregivers who provide individual attention and care. The program strives to create a supportive and loving environment where children can feel safe, valued, and empowered.
